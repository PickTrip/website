We are a passionate team dedicated to transforming the way people plan and experience travel. Founded by Eric, Alex, Ethan (Guanyu), and David, our journey began with a shared enthusiasm for simplifying travel planning. Over the past eight months, we have combined backend development, design, and deep industry expertise to create a new AI-powered travel planning platform that redefines how trips are organized. Planning a trip shouldn’t be time-consuming and exhausting. On average, modern travelers browse 277 web pages and spend 303 minutes just to finalize their itinerary. PickTrip is changing this process. Every line of code we write, every partnership we build, and every product update we release is driven by our commitment to making travel easier and more tailored to each user’s needs.
